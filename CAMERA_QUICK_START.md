# 🚀 Quick Start Guide - AI Fruit Recognition

## How to Use the AI Camera Feature

### Step 1: Access Fruit Learning Mode
1. Open Kid Battle app
2. Click **"🍎 Fruit Learning"** button on main menu

### Step 2: Open AI Camera
1. Inside Fruit Learning Mode
2. Click **"📸 AI Camera"** button (orange/yellow gradient)

### Step 3: Wait for AI Loading
- AI model loads automatically (first time: ~5-10 seconds)
- See "✅ AI Model Loaded!" message

### Step 4: Start Camera
1. Click **"📹 Start Camera"** button
2. Allow camera permissions when browser asks
3. Your camera feed appears on screen

### Step 5: Scan Fruits
1. Point camera at a real fruit
2. Hold steady for 1-2 seconds
3. AI identifies the fruit automatically
4. Hear the fruit name pronounced!
5. See confidence level percentage

### Step 6: Try Different Fruits
- Scan apples, bananas, oranges, lemons, strawberries
- Works best with:
  - Good lighting (natural light is best)
  - Close-up view (fill ~50% of frame)
  - Centered fruit
  - Fruit against plain background

### Step 7: Stop When Done
1. Click **"⏹️ Stop Camera"** to pause
2. Click **"❌ Close"** to return to Fruit Learning

---

## 🎯 Best Practices

### For Best Results:
✅ Use good lighting (natural daylight works best)
✅ Hold fruit close to camera (15-30cm / 6-12 inches)
✅ Keep fruit centered in frame
✅ Use plain background (white, light colors)
✅ Hold camera steady

### Avoid:
❌ Too dark or too bright lighting
❌ Fruit too far from camera
❌ Blurry or shaky images
❌ Cluttered backgrounds
❌ Multiple fruits in frame

## ❓ Troubleshooting

**Camera won't open?**
→ Check browser permissions, reload page

**AI not detecting fruit?**
→ Improve lighting, move fruit closer

**Wrong fruit detected?**
→ Try different angle, remove background clutter

**No sound?**
→ Check device volume, browser audio permissions
